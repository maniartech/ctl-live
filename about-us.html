About
Chemo Test Laboratory

Established in 1984, Chemo Test Laboratory is a government-approved public testing facility serving the pharmaceutical, food & agriculture, cosmetics, and water industries with comprehensive analytical and quality testing solutions. Operating from two independent state-of-the-art units in Sewri and Turbhe, Mumbai, our laboratory is equipped with advanced divisions for chemical analysis, instrumentation, microbiology, sterility testing, and stability studies. Each unit is designed to meet industry-specific requirements, ensuring precise and reliable testing outcomes.

Led by a distinguished family of doctorates, Chemo Test Laboratory has been at the forefront of quality control and analytical testing for over four decades, supporting national and international clients with third-party testing, research, and outsourcing solutions.

Our expertise extends across analytical testing, stability studies, method development & validation, and area validation, alongside physical inspection, surveys, sampling, and sealing services.

About Chemo Test Laboratory

Established in 1984, Chemo Test Laboratory is a government approved public testing laboratory catering the need of Drug & Pharmaceutical, Food & Agricultural, Cosmetics, Water industry for its testing services. The laboratory is having two independent premises having Chemical, Instrumentation, Microbiology, Sterility,Stability studies divisions and equipped with all sophisticated instruments.

Chaired and Managed by a family of Doctorates, Chemo Test Laboratory has been into the Quality control industry for more than 4 decades, supporting various National and overseas companies for their third party testing, research and outsourcing needs.

Chemo Test's business landscape covers analytical testing, stability studies, analytical method development, and validation, area validation, it also covers services like physical inspection, survey, sampling, and sealing.
